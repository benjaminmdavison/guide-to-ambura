Campaign Notes

Reference material for the DM and the party.

Session 5: June 26, 2026

Zoong and Heingveir are aboard the Seadream Chaser. They encountered a band of Kuo-Tua as the ship navigated through the river delta and approached the South Sea.
Later, the befriend the ship's master, Captain Bovious, and his pet monkey, Biggsby.
The ship is attacked by a smaller vessel carrying a crew of about 25 primate pirates: monkeys who have been coerced into villainy by some strange force. The attacking vessel was repelled, and many of the primate pirates were rescued, but the ship was damaged. Zoong helped to repair the ship's rudder, and not a moment too late: the ship barely skimmed the corner of a coral reef that could have caused even bigger problems.

Session 4: June 16-17, 2026

Zoong and Heingveir ventured into The Crossroads and delivered their package to Gunzar. It turns out to be a smuggled spell tome. The party rests at a local inn, only to be caught up in some kind of civil unrest. Imps and a devil were spotted among the affray.
On the journey home, a dragon was spotted in the sky. The city of Porphyrium is abuzz with the news of dragons and the chaos at The Crossroads. The party learned about the lore of the ancient dragons, and learned that a local mad wizard named Faldbar the Red may be responsible for the return of Falt-bath-ar-zet-ghul-pheng, a dreaded Ancient dragon. The party then boarded the Seadream Chaser to journey to St. Alberich.

Session 3: June 10, 2026

Zoong and Heingveir are breaking camp on the road to The Crossroads. They are delivering a bundle of dried herbs and mushrooms to a man named Gunzar, who can be found in the College Row neighborhood in The Crossroads.

Session 2: June 9, 2026

It is late evening. Zoong and Heingveir are getting acquainted in the Wriggling Eel in Porphyrium. They are destined for the land of St. Alberich, and can get there via an overland passage or a sea journey. Zoong is debating finding work, and is considering checking the following places: Sorcerors Enclave Bookgrove Caravan guard work

Session 1: June 2, 2026

Zoong has learned that his “destiny” lies to the East. He has shopped for equipment and is preparing to take a walk by the pond before preparing a goodbye dinner with his family.
