Breland

The prosperous kingdom of Breland occupies the broad valley between the Borovian Mountains and the Dragonback Mountains to the north and south, respectively. It expands from the Peppered Coast in the east to the edge of the grasslands in the west. East-to-west, the kingdom spans about 175 miles; north-to-south, it spans about 120 miles.

The capital city of Breland is the city of Brelas’ Shield, named in honor of an ancient defender. Brelas’ Shield is carved out of a hillside, close to the Borovian Mountains. It sits at the genesis of the River of Diamonds, which flows out of the mountains and heads south. It is located about 75 miles inland from the Peppered Coast and about 25 miles from the edge of the Borovian Mountains.

About 75 miles downriver from Brelas’ Shield is The Screelands, a town carved out of jagged and weird rock formations. It is known for good metal and exceptional meat. To the east of the Screelands is a wide span of uninhabitable marshland that eventually runs into the sea.

The River of Diamonds turns west at the Screelands and flows for approximately 50 miles to meet The Crossroads, a fast-growing city located at a natural junction of land routes that saddles the north side of the river. Farther west, the River of Diamonds meets the muddy River Twain at the Free City of Porphyrium. These are the major cities of Breland.

A smattering of small villages and settlements dots the landscape. Most of these villages are simple towns built around resources like timber, ore, or farmland; some have sprouted up from ancient campsites or inns. Travelers to the Kingdom usually go to the cities, as there is not much entertainment in the villages.

Breland is a homeland for humans. The wide basin that Breland occupies was never settled by any other groups, and the humans have built a thriving kingdom in the land. However, members of other species coexist with the humans, mostly in the larger cities. The villages and settlements are predominantly human.

Breland’s prevailing way of life emphasizes mutual tolerance and cooperation. Everybody loves dwarf-forged metals, elf-sewn garments, and gnome-built artifacts. The Dwarves, Elves, and Gnomes, gifted with longer lives than those of humans, often build successful businesses and end up employing humans. A fair number of wealthy nobles in Breland are not human, which is sometimes a point of contention for the less tolerant humans.

The Kingdom of Breland is currently ruled by King Philip Wyrmbane the Fair and his wife, Queen Scylla Wyrmbane the Kind. They have five sons and a daughter: Henri Wyrmbane the Just, Barkus Wyrmbane, Lord of the Screeland, Bors Wyrmbane the Bold, Lord of the Crossroads, Hyacinth Wyrmbane the Beautiful, Geoffrey Wyrmbane, the Strong, and Lukas Wyrmbane the Loyal, Prince of the Realm. Their children act as royal proxies in the cities and generally act as royalty at large. Henri, first in line for the throne, is often sent to other kingdoms as a diplomat and groomed for his eventual ascension.
